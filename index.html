Privacy Policy

At Bytsea Statement Parser, we prioritize your privacy and data security. Here's how we protect your information:

Data Privacy

This app processes financial data entirely within your browser. Your data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

API Usage

The Google Gemini API is used for AI-powered data extraction. Your personal API key and data are stored only in your browser's local storage and are used only for communicating directly with Google's services.

Data Security

We do not track, store, or share any of your financial information or uploaded documents. You retain full control of your data at all times.
